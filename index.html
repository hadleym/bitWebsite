Billy launched the BIT’s legacy podcasts rabbitHOLE and Podcast Town in June 2024, and the inaugural episode of MOVIE! MOVIE! is expected just in time for Spooky Season. Also in the BIT pod pipeline is the BIT SPOTLIGHT which will serve as BIT’s experimental stage. Look for iconic team reunions, edgy indie teams, and BIT Crew flexes. Our ever-growing ensemble is called the BIT CREW. CREW members cycle through four weeks of workshops and then a week of podcast recording. Editing, art, publishing, and distribution are all handled by CREW members. Please listen, rate, and share your favorite BIT Pods with your friends and family.
