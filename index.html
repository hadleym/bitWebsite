The Podcasts

Improv pioneer and character actor Billy Merritt realized his lifelong dream of creating a comedy theater. There are no dusty boards to tread or squeaky pullies to pull. The BIT COMEDY Network lives in the cloud—it's virtual, international, and showcases the next generation of improv stars.

The BIT Comedy Network is an online, international community of improvisers dedicated to refining their craft through workshops and performances of some the most challenging forms. Billy Merritt is living out his dream of creating a virtual “Theatre Ensemble.”
