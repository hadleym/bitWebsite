About Billy Merritt

Billy Merritt’s entertainment career as an actor, writer, teacher, and producer spans three decades and three markets (New York, Los Angeles, and South Florida).

Billy's affiliation with Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre began at its inception. He created numerous UCB shows, including The Documentary, The Improvised One Acts, and Murder Murder.

Billy started a successful TV and voice acting career on UCB’s Comedy Central show, Late Night with Conan O’Brien, and VH1’s Best Week Ever.

In Los Angeles, Billy booked shows like The Connors, Reno 911, Parks and Recreation, Brooklyn Nine-Nine, and more.

Billy returned to Florida in 2023, proudly maintaining a "Florida Man" lifestyle while performing in Florida, Los Angeles, and the EU.
